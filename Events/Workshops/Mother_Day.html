About

The Cake Deco Club open its doors to any UoM student or staffs in a decoration workshop for Mother's Day on Friday 12 May 2023 from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m.

Venue: The Cooking Room, University of Mauritius

Applications open up till Sunday 7 May 2023 (Limited seats Available)

Interested person to send contact details on Cakedecoclub@umail.ac.mu

On Friday 12 May 2023, the following cakes will be done to learn more about the different techniques of cake decoration:

1. Mother's Day Airbrushed flower Cake

Technique to be learned: Airbrushing technique

2. Mother's Day cake pops

Technique to be learned: Simple decoration (On-trend technique)

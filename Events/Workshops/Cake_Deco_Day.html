About

The Cake Deco Club open its doors to any UoM student or staffs in a decoration workshop for the National Cake Decoration Day on Tuesday 10 October 2023 from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m.

To celebrate the National Cake Decoration Day, a workshop competition will be organized to allow students or staffs to showcase their skills in cake decoration through the various techniques available.

The Winner will win a Cake Decoration Kit with tools especially chosen by our club members

Venue: The Cooking Room, University of Mauritius

Applications open up till Thursday 5 October 2023 (Limited seats Available)

Interested person to send contact details on Cakedecoclub@umail.ac.mu

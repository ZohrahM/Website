About

The Cake Deco Club open its doors to any UoM student or staffs in a decoration workshop for Christmas on Thursday 22 December 2022 and Friday 23 December 2022 from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m

Venue: The Cooking Room, University of Mauritius

Applications open up till Sunday 18 December 2022 (Limited seats Available)

Interested person to send contact details on Cakedecoclub@umail.ac.mu

For more Information about the different workshops organized this year and the general rule and requirement, click here

Cakes & Techniques

On the 2-day workshop, the following cakes will be done to learn more about the different techniques of cake decoration:

1. Surprise Cake Christmas tree Cake

Technique to be learned: Carved Cakes technique

2. Cute Snowman Cake

Technique to be learned: Carved Cakes technique

3. Naked Christmas Cake

Technique to be learned: Aesthetic cake (On-trend technique)

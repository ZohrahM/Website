About

The Cake Deco Club open its doors to any UoM student or staffs in a decoration workshop for St Valentine's Day on Monday 13 February 2023 from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m.

Venue: The Cooking Room, University of Mauritius

Applications open up till Wednesday 8 February 2023 (Limited seats Available)

Interested person to send contact details on Cakedecoclub@umail.ac.mu

For more Information about the different workshops organized this year and the general rule and requirement, click here

Cakes & Techniques

On Monday 13 February 2023, the following cakes will be done to learn more about the different techniques of cake decoration:

1. Petal Fantasy Flowers on Metallic cake

Technique to be learned: Metallic effect technique

2. Valentine's Cakesicles

Technique to be learned: Simple decoration (On-trend technique)

3. Painted Rose Cake

Technique to be learned: Hand Painted cake technique

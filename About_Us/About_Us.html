Our Club

The Cake Decoration Club is a university club created to make anyone interested, discover the world of cake decoration. Our goal is to make more and more people interested in the different cake decoration techniques to embellish their cakes.

Despite our club targeting only UoM students and staff at the moment, we aim to expand one day to make our club and workshops available to a greater audience!
